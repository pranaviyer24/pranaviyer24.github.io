About me

My interest in design started in college, when I dabbled with Adobe Illustrator and designed posters for various student clubs. After graduating I learnt about the field of UX and I fell in love with it because it combined technology, and creative thinking. It was also an opportunity to research and understand people and how they behave.

I am a problem solver and a quick learner and am passionate about the accessibility of technology and empathetic design.
